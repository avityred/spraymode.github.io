What is free software?

"Free software means the users have the freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software. Free software is a matter of liberty, not price. To understand the concept, you should think of “free” as in “free speech”, not as in “free beer”."


Proprietary (non-free) software is almost always spyware.

It's been proven that most proprietary software tracks and spies on it's users.

Everything you type, every message you send, even your exact coordinates are being tracked.


Take a stance against proprietary software.
