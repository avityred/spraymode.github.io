Application Benefits Disadvantages Conclusion
Discord Extremely Popular, Easy to use. Proprietary, tracks a lot of unnecessary data. Company has a very shady past and it also tracks a lot of your data. Avoid it.
Instagram Very popular, doubles as image-sharing platform. Proprietary, tracks a lot of unnecessary data and requires camera access at all times. Obvious spyware, owned by facebook. Avoid it.
Skype Popular among businesses, relatively easy to use. Proprietary, tracks a lot of unnecessary data. Avoid it, not only does it track you but it's owned by microsoft.
TeamSpeak High quality voice chat, new beta client extremely easy to use and fast. Proprietary. Just avoid it.
IRC Multiple clients available, more secure than many chatting software. Not easy to use, not very popular. Use if you like, but it's likely not worth your time, because Mumble and Matrix have more features.
Mumble Open Source, high Quality voice chat. Not easy to use. If you are fine with giving up some user friendliness in turn for efficiency, then use Mumble.
GNU Jami Free, extremely Secure. Not very popular. GNU Jami is great, but it's more suitable for business.
Matrix Free, mutlple clients available, extremely secure, easy to use. Not very popular. If you are looking for something easy to use, with tons of features then go with this.
